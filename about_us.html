About Us

About Us 關於我們

香港科技大學學生會工學院學生會(ESU) 是一個在香港科技大學的非牟利學生團體。ESU代表逾3000名就讀科大的工程學系的本科生及研究生。
ESU一直致力於向所有會員提供各種服務。包括會員及科大工程學生的福利、學術活動及課外活動﹐並藉此推廣科大工程的正面形象。

The Engineering Students' Union, HKUSTSU (ESU) is a non-profit student organisation in The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST).
ESU represents around 3000 undergraduates and postgraduates of School of Engineering. ESU aims to promote welfare, academic, extra-curricular activities to members and engineering students. ESU also promotes a positive image of Engineering Studies at HKUST.

Join Us 加入我們

ESU鼓勵所有合資格的科大學生﹐尤其是就讀工程的學生加入我們﹐成為我們的會員。
ESU提供一系列的福利及活動予ESU各會員。 不要猶豫了﹐請於開課期間親臨ESU counter 或 電郵致 su_esu@connect.ust.hk 加入我們﹐成為我們的一分子。

ESU encourages all eligible HKUST students, especialy engineering students to join us and become our members. ESU provide a series of welfare and activities to our members. Please don't hesitate, join us by coming to ESU counter during semester OR email to su_esu@connect.ust.hk
