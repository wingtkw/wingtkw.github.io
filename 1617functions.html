Orientation Camp 16-18/08/2016



即將寫下人生新一頁的你，準備好了嗎？作為新生難免要經歷迷惘的時期。但切記不要讓迷惘阻礙你們的步伐，作為一名工程系學生，必須具備迎難而上的精神。工學院學生會(ESU)作為服務工程系學生的團體，願意擔當迷霧中的燈塔，協助各位新生渡過艱難如斯的時期。與其整日埋首於沉重的學業壓力，不如抽些許時間參加我們為大家準備的活動，擴寬視野和建立社交圈子，更重要的是能夠重拾歡樂! 學懂獨立並不代表要孤立自己；相反，擴寬社交圈子乃是踏入社會甚至人生中的重要一環。我們舉辦的迎新營(O-Camp)正是你們結識良朋益友的絕佳場所，三日兩夜的營期，放開自己並與組爸媽以及其他新生變得熟稔吧!

For all freshmen that just joined HKUST, ESU is now presenting you our O-Camp. A Camp that allows you to know the other freshmen. They are your friends, best friends, our futhure classmate, project partner. As an organisation serving for Engineering Studnet, ESU acts as a bridge to connect all people. Don't hesitate, come and join it.

白色"程"人節 14/03/2016



The White Valentine’s Day was held on 14th-16th March, 2016, to establish a caring image of ESU and to enhance good relationship between students of the Engineering School and the Union. A special counter was set on LG5 for members to take photos and members drawn Omikuji and Omamori as souvenis. Some ExCOs delievered roses randomly on Academic Concourse outside LT-B. Wish all students in HKUST could have a nice White Valentine’s Day.

The 22nd Inauguration Ceremony 25/02/2016



The 22nd inauguration Ceremony signifies the start of the 22nd Executive Committee of ESU. It was held sucessfully on 25th Feburary, 2016. Ir. Chan Chi Chiu (President of The Hong Kong Institution of Engineers), Prof. Tongxi Yu (Former Acting Dean of School of Engineering), Ms. Natalie Lam(The President of ESU,HKUSTSU, Session 2015-2016), together with professional engineers, guests and society representatives from HKUST and other universities have been witnessing our ceremony and gave us a lot of support.
