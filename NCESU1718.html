Engineering Mentoring Programme 2016 11/2016


We aim to provide a platform for engineering students to get in touch with professional engineers working on the field. Have you ever felt uncertain about building your career as an engineer or unclear about the difference between different majors? Through this program, you can know more about the working environment, daily duties and career pathway as different engineers. Nevertheless, we provide opportunity for job shadowing which can let you experience the work as an engineer. We believe that it will be helpful for preparing your future career or for major selection.

Benefits of the joining EMP

  • A Program for you to know Engineers in the industries
  • A platform to connect with the excellent engineers in Hong Kong
  • Get to know what it is like to be an engineer
  • Equip yourself
