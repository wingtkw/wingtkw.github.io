The 24th Inauguration Ceremony 22/2/2018



The 24th inauguration Ceremony denotes the commencement of the 24th Executive Committee of ESU. It was held successfully on 22nd February, 2018. Ir Thomas Chan Kwok Cheung (President of The Hong Kong Institution of Engineers), Prof. Tim Kwang Ting Cheng (Dean of School of Engineering), Mr. Chan Ka Hei Philson (President of ESU,HKUSTSU, Session 2017-2018), together with professional engineers, guests and society representatives from HKUST and other universities have been participating and witnessing our ceremony. It is our honor to have your support in this memorable night.
